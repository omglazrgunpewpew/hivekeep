01Persistent

They never forget you.

No "new conversation". One continuous session, and a hybrid memory that builds context over months. Nothing is deleted: old turns are summarized, never thrown away.

A Agent hands work to another, or spawns sub-agents to move faster. The team shares one history and one address book.

02Sovereign

One container. It improves itself.

No Postgres, Redis, Mongo, or message broker. Bun and SQLite in a single binary. Your data, your keys, your machine.

The platform extends itself: your Agents write tools, build mini-apps, and ship plugins. Secrets live in an encrypted vault and never reach the LLM.

$ docker run -d -p 3000:3000 hivekeep
# then open your browser. Queenie does the rest.

03Setup

Setup is a conversation, not a YAML file.

Queenie, your setup agent, connects your providers, secures your secrets, and creates your first Agents by chatting with you. It stays around for good.

Tools render as real, themed UI in the thread — a weather card, not raw JSON. Mini-apps become dashboards inside Hivekeep.
